Welcome to Mountains Contractor

Your Trusted Partner in Building Dreams Since 2004

Mountains Contractor, founded and led by Murtuja Ansari, has been a cornerstone of quality construction in Dehradun for over two decades. With 20 years of dedicated service, we have established ourselves as the most reliable name in residential construction and home renovation.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to build homes that are not just structures, but spaces where families create lasting memories. Today, we stand proud with hundreds of successfully completed projects and countless satisfied homeowners.
